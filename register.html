Information

How to submit

There are two different submission forms. To submit your ABSTRACT, click on "Submit abstract button" and you will be taken to the corresponding form to fill. Abstracts must have a maximum length of 250 words.
This workshop, in addition to offering a favorable environment for young researchers to present their results, will give the opportunity to join a presentation of the various equipments present in the HERCULES laboratory. Additionally, a practical workshop in the form of 3 sessions will be proposed in order to present XRF, XRD and FTIR. After a short presentation of the questions that the participants are confronted with on their samples, samples from the approved projects will be selected to be analyzed free of charge with the methods presented during the workshop.
